Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18

TruthLens ("we", "the service") is a tool that analyzes news screenshots and provides an AI-generated credibility assessment. This policy explains what data we process and how.

1. What we process

  • The image you upload — the news screenshot you submit for analysis.
  • Your language preference — stored locally in your browser, not on our servers.
  • We do not ask for your name, email, account, or any personal identifier to use the tool.

2. How your image is used

  • Your uploaded image is sent to a third-party AI service (Alibaba Cloud Bailian / Qwen, with servers located in mainland China) to identify the source and assess credibility.
  • Relevant keywords or titles extracted from the image may be sent to the Brave Search API to gather corroborating evidence.
  • Images are processed in memory only. We do not store your images on our servers or in any database, and we do not keep them after the analysis returns.

3. Third-party services

  • Alibaba Cloud (Bailian / DashScope) — AI image and text analysis.
  • Brave Search API — evidence search.
  • Advertising (if enabled) — ad providers such as Google AdSense may set cookies to serve and measure ads.

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. Because the AI provider's servers are in mainland China, do not upload images containing sensitive personal information.

4. Cookies & local storage

  • We use your browser's local storage only to remember your language choice.
  • If advertising is enabled, ad providers may use cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Accuracy & disclaimer

Results are AI-generated and for reference only. They are not a factual determination, nor an accusation against any individual or organization, and may be inaccurate. Always verify important information with official sources.

6. Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

1. 我们处理哪些信息

  • 你上传的图片——即你提交分析的新闻截图。
  • 你的语言偏好——保存在你浏览器本地，不上传到我们服务器。
  • 使用本工具无需提供姓名、邮箱、账号或任何身份信息。

2. 你的图片如何被使用

  • 你上传的图片会被发送给第三方 AI 服务（阿里云百炼 / 通义千问，服务器位于中国大陆）用于识别来源、评估可信度。
  • 从图片中提取的关键词或标题，可能被发送给 Brave Search 搜索接口，用于查找佐证信息。
  • 图片仅在内存中处理。我们不会把你的图片存到服务器或任何数据库，分析返回后即不再保留。

3. 第三方服务

  • 阿里云（百炼 / DashScope）——AI 图像与文本分析。

上述服务按其各自的隐私政策处理数据。由于 AI 服务商服务器位于中国大陆，请勿上传含敏感个人信息的图片。

4. Cookie 与本地存储

  • 我们仅使用浏览器本地存储来记住你的语言选择。
  • 如启用广告，广告商可能使用 Cookie。你可在浏览器设置中管理 Cookie。

分析结果由 AI 生成，仅供参考，不代表事实认定，也不构成对任何个人或机构的指控，可能存在错误。重要信息请以官方发布为准。
